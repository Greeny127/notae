## **General information**
The [[House of Capet]] was the ruling house of France, from 987 to 1328. Founded by Hugh Capet, in 987, the House was in power uninterrupted till 1328 when the [[House of Valois]] and the [[House of Bourbons]] took over.

## **Capetian kings of France (987 - 1328)**
0987–0996: [[Hugh Capet]]
0996–1031: [[Robert II the Pious]] (crowned associate king 987)
1031–1060: [[Henry I]] (crowned associate king 1027)
1060–1108: [[Philip I]] (crowned associate king 1059)
1108–1137: [[Louis VI]] the Fat (crowned associate king 1102)
1137–1180: [[Louis VII]] (crowned associate king 1131)
1180–1223: [[Philip II Augustus]] (crowned associate king 1179)

1226–1270: [[Saint Louis IX]]
1270–1285: [[Philip III the Bold]]
