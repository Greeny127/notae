Ancien Regime can be defined as the political and social system in place before the [[French Revolution|1789 revolution]], starting from the late Middle Ages.

Notable features of this age:
- Ruled by the [[Capetian Dynasty]]
- High local autonomy
- Strong decentralization of law and power
- Significant regional differences
- Difficult trade barriers within the country
- Large social divide between and within the [[The Three Estates of Pre-Revolutionary France|three estates]].
